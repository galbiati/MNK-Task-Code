Rules

1. You can use ore to buy miners, builders, or armies. Each costs 1 ore.

2. Each turn, you get an amount of ore equal to the number of miners.

3. At the end of your turn, your walls grow by the number of your builders, and your opponent's walls shrink by the number of your armies.

4. Additionally, half the size of your army (rounded down) is subtracted from the opponent's number of builders and miners. You then lose half your army (rounded down).

5. You win when either your opponent's walls are reduced to 0 or when your own walls are increased to 20.
